About This Class

The class community space for Processes and Workflows: Visual Interaction Design (FA/YSDN 2005), a second year course in the Bachelor of Design Program offered jointly between York University and Sheridan College. This site serves as central communication point for course projects, updates, scheduling, resources, and other relevant material that informs and promotes class activities.
